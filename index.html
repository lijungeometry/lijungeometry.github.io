Research:

I am interested in symplectic topology, complex analysis, 3 and 4 dim-topology, and dynamical systems;
in particular, the mapping class groups and dynamics of symplectic manifolds, in relation to mirror symmetry and symplectic/Lagrangian embedding problems.
My work also covers the applications in information geometry, which combines ideas from analysis, geometry and statistics;
in particular, symplectic techniques such as moment maps can be applied to statistical models with symmetry (rigid body motion for example) and Riemannian Neural Networks.
